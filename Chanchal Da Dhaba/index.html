Rasgulla

Ingreadent

  • 2 litre milk
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
  • 3 tablespoon lime juice

Racipe

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice or vinegar.
  2. Strain and rinse the curds to remove the sour taste.
  3. Knead the curds into a smooth dough.
  4. Make small balls from the dough and set aside.
  5. Boil sugar and water to make the syrup.
  6. Cook the dough balls in the syrup, cool, and refrigerate before serving.
      7. Enjoy your homemade Rasgullas!

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup milk (approximately)
  • ghee for deep frying

Racipe

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and ghee.
  2. Gradually add milk and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls, ensuring no cracks on the surface.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan on low heat.
  5. Fry the dough balls until golden brown and drain excess oil.
  6. Soak the hot Gulab Jamun in warm sugar syrup for 1-2 hours.
      7. Enjoy your delicious homemade Gulab Jamun
